About Me

T J Alok is currently pursuing an LLM in International Trade Law at the National University of Advanced Legal Studies (NUALS), Kochi. He completed his LLB from Madhusudan Law University and holds a BSc in Chemistry (Honours) from Berhampur University with a stellar CGPA of 9.3.

He was honoured with the Student of the Year Award for academic excellence. His interdisciplinary journey, spanning the natural sciences and law, equips him with a uniquely analytical perspective.
